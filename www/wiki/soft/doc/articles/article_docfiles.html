doc files

To add resource files in your wiki source, you must put them in a "doc-files" sub-directory anywhere in the wiki source. These directories won't be searched for wiki XML files.

For example:
      wiki
      ==> resourceDef.xml
      ==> doc-files
      ====> myResource.xml
      ====> anotherResource.html
Note that you don't need to put images in a "doc-files" directories, because of course images are no XML files and as such the parser will not try to parse them.
